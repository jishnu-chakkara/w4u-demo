Hands-On GST Training

A practical, experience-based program designed to equip learners with real-time skills in Goods and Services Tax (GST) compliance and operations. This training covers essential aspects such as GST registration, invoice creation, input tax credit (ITC) claims, return filing (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, etc.), and reconciliation using industry-standard tools and software. Participants will work on live scenarios and case studies, gaining the confidence to manage GST processes efficiently in a professional setting. Ideal for accounting students, professionals, and business owners looking to stay GST-compliant and boost their practical knowledge.
