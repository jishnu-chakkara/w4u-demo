Course Overview

"Advanced Professional Accounting Course, Its a comprehensive program designed to equip experienced individuals with in-depth knowledge and practical skills in complex accounting principles, financial management, auditing, taxation, and financial reporting. This course targets professionals seeking to enhance their expertise, preparing them for senior roles in accounting and finance, and focusing on advanced topics such as international accounting standards, forensic accounting, and strategic financial planning."

AHP

Practical Training in Business Accounting: A hands-on program designed to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world accounting practices. This course offers in-depth training on day-to-day accounting tasks such as journal entries, ledger maintenance, financial statement preparation, bank reconciliation, GST compliance, and payroll processing. Ideal for aspiring accountants and finance professionals, the training uses real-life scenarios and accounting software to build job-ready skills and ensure confidence in handling business financial operations.

AHP PRIME

A practical, experience-based program designed to equip learners with real-time skills in Goods and Services Tax (GST) compliance and operations. This training covers essential aspects such as GST registration, invoice creation, input tax credit (ITC) claims, return filing (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, etc.), and reconciliation using industry-standard tools and software. Participants will work on live scenarios and case studies, gaining the confidence to manage GST processes efficiently in a professional setting. Ideal for accounting students, professionals, and business owners looking to stay GST-compliant and boost their practical knowledge.

AHP PLUS

This practical Income Tax training program offers step-by-step guidance on Income Tax portal registration and individual income tax return filing. Gain hands-on experience with depreciation calculation, books of account, and documentation requirements under the Income Tax Act. The process for PAN and TAN applications. Learn to manage TDS, TCS, and Advance Tax provisions, including return filing and compliance procedures. Get practical knowledge on interpreting Form 26AS and the Annual Information Statement (AIS). Master TDS calculation, TDS return filing, and Form 16 generation using real-life scenarios and tools.
